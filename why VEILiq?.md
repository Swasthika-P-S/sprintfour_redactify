# VEILIQ – Think Before You Share

## About the Project

Today, millions of people use AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and other online platforms to improve resumes, summarize medical reports, review legal documents, or translate files. However, most users unknowingly upload sensitive personal information such as phone numbers, Aadhaar numbers, bank details, addresses, and student IDs.
Existing solutions mainly focus on detecting and redacting Personally Identifiable Information (PII), but privacy is much more than hiding names. Even after redaction, a person can often be identified through indirect information such as their university, city, profession, or organization.

That's why we built **VEILIQ**.
VEILIQ is an AI-powered, context-aware privacy assistant that helps users make informed decisions before sharing documents. It not only detects and redacts sensitive information but also analyzes the risk of re-identification, applies context-specific privacy policies (Education, Healthcare, HR, Banking, etc.), and provides recommendations based on the intended use of the document.
### Our Goal

Our goal is to help users **Think Before They Share**, ensuring that documents remain useful while protecting personal identity, maintaining privacy, and building trust in AI-assisted document sharing.
